Hello! I am an passionate Biostatistics M.S. candidate at Emory University, also holding a M.S. degree in Computer Sciences and a B.S. in Bioinformatics. My expertise lies in data analysis, machine learning, Python, and SAS programming, which I currently apply in pioneering research in spatial statistics and transcriptomic data analysis. I also have extensive experience in R programming, having developed an R package and its corresponding R Shiny App. Constantly learning and not limiting myself to my field, I am committed to using my skills to develop solutions for rare/complex diseases, cancer, and disabilities, aiming to make a significant impact in the biomedical field.
